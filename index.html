Information About me

I'm Michal Nithesh, a passionate aspiring web developer based in Nagercoil, Kanyakumari, Tamil Nadu. Armed with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering, specialized in the MERN stack, from Stella Mary's College of Engineering, I'm on a mission to craft visually appealing and functional websites. As a member of Byte-Bash-Blitz at my alma mater, I actively engaged in technical pursuits.
Proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and Python, I bring a versatile skill set to the table. Fluent in both Tamil and English, I navigate projects seamlessly. My toolset includes Git, GitHub, VSCode, Bootstrap, CodePen, and Jupyter.
Among my notable projects are the development of a Caesar Cipher tool, a captivating Quiz Game, and an engaging Tic Tac Toe Game, all showcasing my prowess in web development.
I hold a JavaScript Course Completion Certificate from Scaler Academy and am currently undertaking the 365 Days Code Challenge with the same institution. With a commitment to continuous learning and a passion for innovation, I am poised to make meaningful contributions in the dynamic realm of web development.
